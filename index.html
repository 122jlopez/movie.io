Creed II
Release Date: November 21,2018

A boxer named Adonis Creed excepts the challange of fighting against Ivan Dragos son to seek revenge for the death of his father. In 1985 Ivan Drago Killed Adonis' father Apollo Creed in a brutal boxing match. With his trainer Rocky Balboa he prepares to fight the most important fight of his life to regain his fathers legacy and his own pride.
